Shrimps Pasta

Ingredients

  • Shrimps
  • Cream sauce
  • Olive oil
  • Butter
  • Lemon
  • Garlic
  • Parmesan cheese
  • spinach
  • Italian herbs
  • Salt and pepper
  • Red chili flakes
  1. Season the shrimps with salt pepper and red chili flakes and italian herbs
  2. squeeze half a lemon and garlic
  3. mix them together
  4. boil the pasta in a pan aside
  5. add salt and water
  6. in a hot pan add butter and olive oil
  7. add spinach and shrimps
  8. wait 7 minutes until its golden
  9. when done put shrimps aside
  10. in the same pan add cream and parmesan cheese
  11. when the cream is boiled add all the ingredients
  12. add the pasta and mix them all
  13. season them with salt and pepper

    14. bon appetit
